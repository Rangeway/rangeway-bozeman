Premium charging is coming to Bozeman.

Minutes from Bozeman Yellowstone International Airport at the I-90 Exit 299 interchange, Rangeway is building a charging stop worth looking forward to.

Premium EV charging meets luxury hospitality. Travel farther, stop better.

01

The Promise

Charging should not mean waiting in your car.

Every Rangeway Waystation and Basecamp guarantees climate-controlled indoor comfort. That means a real driver's lounge with premium restrooms, comfortable seating, and high-speed Wi-Fi, ready whether you are passing through or starting a long drive.

No other charging network in America makes this promise.

i.

Climate-controlled driver's lounge, comfortable in every season.

ii.

Premium restrooms and high-speed Wi-Fi, every visit.

iii.

Quality coffee and food, made for the time you are here.

02

The Experience

A stop you look forward to.

Pull in, plug in, and step inside. While your vehicle charges on one of eight ultra-fast charging ports, you settle into a mobile lounge built the way a hospitality operator designs a space, with thoughtful lighting, comfortable seating, and good coffee. The charging happens around a place built for the people waiting, not in a lot built for the car.

03

The Location

Right where the journey needs it.

The location sits at the I-90 Exit 299 interchange in Belgrade, next door to Bozeman Yellowstone International Airport, one of the busiest and fastest-growing airports in the region. The Bozeman area is among the fastest-growing communities in Montana, and reliable fast charging here has been hard to find. We are changing that.

What's Coming

Built to become more.

Rangeway Bozeman opens as a premium charging plaza with a mobile lounge and great coffee and food.

It is designed to grow into a full Basecamp, a staffed destination with an expanded lounge and an on-site cafe. This is the first step on the trail, not the last.

05

Why Rangeway

We think like hotel operators, not utility companies.

Rangeway was founded by a hospitality operator with more than 15 years in luxury hospitality operations, alongside years inside the EV driver community. Every location is designed the way a great property is designed, around the experience of the people inside it. That is the difference you will feel the moment you pull in.

"The stop should be worth the time. We design every Rangeway location around the person who just stepped out of the car, because that is who we are building for."

Zak Winnick · Founder and CEO

The Partners

The team behind Rangeway Bozeman.

We bring in best-in-class partners for every part of the experience.

Electric Era

Charging and storage

Electric Era's new RetailEdge dispensers power charging at the plaza, backed by on-site battery storage that keeps every session quick and reliable.

LouTeq

Structures

LouTeq designs and builds the canopies and structures that shelter the plaza, giving the site durable, design-forward architecture.

Pebble

Driver's lounge

Pebble provides the mobile driver's lounge, a climate-controlled space where drivers and guests can relax while they charge.

Rivian

Food and coffee

Rivian provides the electric food truck on the plaza, run by a local operator serving fresh food and coffee on site.
