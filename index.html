The promise

Charging should not mean waiting in your car.

Every Rangeway Waystation and Basecamp guarantees climate-controlled indoor comfort. That means a real driver's lounge with premium restrooms, comfortable seating, and high-speed Wi-Fi, ready whether you are passing through or starting a long drive. No other charging network in America makes this promise.

Climate-controlled driver's lounge, comfortable in every season.

Premium restrooms and high-speed Wi-Fi, every visit.

Where we are

Right where the journey needs it.

The location sits at the I-90 Exit 299 interchange in Belgrade, next door to Bozeman Yellowstone International Airport, one of the busiest and fastest-growing airports in the region. The Bozeman area is among the fastest-growing communities in Montana, and reliable fast charging here has been hard to find. We are changing that.

  • At the I-90 Exit 299 interchange
  • Next to the airport
  • Easy on, easy off

The experience

A stop you look forward to.

Pull in, plug in, and step inside. While your vehicle charges on one of eight ultra-fast charging ports, you settle into a mobile lounge built the way a hospitality operator designs a space, with thoughtful lighting, comfortable seating, and good coffee. The charging happens around a place built for the people waiting, not in a lot built for the car.

Eight ultra-fast charging ports

Eight ultra-fast charging ports, designed for a quick, reliable charge so you spend your time inside, not watching a screen.

A mobile lounge built for comfort

A climate-controlled mobile lounge with comfortable seating and high-speed Wi-Fi, so the wait feels like a break rather than a chore.

Coffee and food worth the stop

Locally roasted coffee and quality food on site, served for the people who are here right now, not pulled from a vending machine.

Solar-supported, clean, and well lit

Solar-supported canopies and a clean, well-lit design that reads as a place you want to be, day or night.

What's coming

Built to become more.

Rangeway Bozeman opens as a premium charging plaza with a mobile lounge and great coffee and food. It is designed to grow into a full Basecamp, a staffed destination with an expanded lounge and an on-site cafe. This is the first step on the trail, not the last.

Why Rangeway

We think like hotel operators, not utility companies.

Rangeway was founded by a hospitality operator with more than 15 years in luxury hospitality operations, alongside years inside the EV driver community. Every location is designed the way a great property is designed, around the experience of the people inside it. That is the difference you will feel the moment you pull in.

"The stop should be worth the time. We design every Rangeway location around the person who just stepped out of the car, because that is who we are building for."

Zak Winnick, Founder and CEO
